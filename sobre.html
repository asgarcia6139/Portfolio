ABOUT US

LMNT was created by Alexis Garcia as a new wave of coffee products. The coffee came to life when he had a vision for making coffee a commitment not only as a daily fuel but also as a lifestyle. He believes the name has potential in making a change not only in how coffee is consumed but also the environment that it displays for people.
